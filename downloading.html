Downloading from Riff.CC

How do I download on desktop?

We currently suggest using WebTorrent as it offers a great experience and even allows you to watch the content while it downloads!

You can also use BiglyBT, qBitTorrent or Deluge if you're after a little more power.

How do I download on Android?

Use Flud on Android devices for the best experience. You can use the free version or the paid version.

How do I download on iOS?(iPhone and iPad)

Currently iPhone™ and iPad™ are not officially supported for downloading, but we are working on bringing in-browser streaming so you can use the site anyways. Until then, you can still use the site, you just won't be able to download things.

How do I download on consoles?(Xbox, PlayStation)

Currently Xbox™ and PlayStation™ are not officially supported for downloading, but we are working on bringing in-browser streaming so you can use the site anyways. Until then, you can still use the site, you just won't be able to download things.
