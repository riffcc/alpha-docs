Frequently Asked Questions

What is this?

This is Riff.CC, a site for downloading and sharing anything legally free and interesting. We're completely open source and lots of fun. We're run by Benjamin at the moment but the Riff.CC Foundation is being formed as we speak.

How do you achieve fast download speeds?

Simply put, we take some of the best technology normally used for piracy and use it for legitimate purposes. BitTorrent power!

What is BON? (Bonus Points)

BON is the currency of Riff.CC. It can be earned by uploading content, helping the site out, or leaving your downloader open to contribute to our global distribution network. Soon we will allow artists and users to sell their own merchandise on the site in exchange for BON - and we take absolutely no cut.

You will also eventually be able to trade BON for cryptocurrency. More information on this will be announced soon.

In the future we will also be moving BON to some sort of blockchain - likely SLP or Coloured Coins.

Why?

Our mission is to bring free culture to the world and unlock the potential of the previously unseen. We want to enable artists and poets, philosophers and coders around the world to find an audience and simply focus on the act of creation.
