UNIT3D Article System

In this page, you will find frequently asked questions about the article/news system in UNIT3D. If you have a question and couldn't find it here, just visit our discord!

  1. What is this articles system?
  2. How do I make a article?
  3. Where are articles displayed?
What is this articles system?

An article is like a news announcement you want to post for all members of your site to see.

For example you want to announce planned maintaince, a new feature or alike. Simply make a news article.

How do I make a article?

Simply visit your staff dashboard and click Articles located in the dashboard menu under General Tools.

A article only requires 3 simple things. A title, the content and a image.

Where are articles displayed?

Articles are displayed on home page of your site.
